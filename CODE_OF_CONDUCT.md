# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and constructive community.

Examples of positive behavior:

- Assume good intent and communicate with clarity
- Give feedback on ideas without attacking people
- Share context, tradeoffs, and evidence when disagreeing
- Help others learn the protocol and contribute safely

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, intimidation, or sustained disruption
- Publishing private information without permission
- Bad-faith or hostile participation

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact [info@agentoffernetwork.com](mailto:info@agentoffernetwork.com). All reports will be handled confidentially.
